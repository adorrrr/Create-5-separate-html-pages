About Netflix Clone

Netflix Clone is a website that offers streaming of movies and TV shows. It is a clone of the popular streaming service Netflix. The website is designed to provide a similar user experience to the original Netflix website. Users can browse through movies and TV shows, create an account, and manage their subscriptions.
